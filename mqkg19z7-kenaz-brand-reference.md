## 2. Typography

- **Primary typeface:** **Raleway**, weight **300 (Light)** — used for BOTH headings and body.
- **Fallback stack:** `Raleway, "Nunito Sans", "Open Sans", sans-serif`
- **Base body size:** ~112.5% (≈18px), light weight, normal style.
- **Google Fonts import:**
  ```html
  <link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Raleway:300,400,600&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
  ```
- **Feel:** light, airy, modern, lots of letter-spacing on uppercase labels. Keep weights thin; the brand reads delicate, not bold.

## 4. Voice & Tone

**Characteristics:** inspirational, transformational, spiritual-yet-grounded, warm, professional, community-minded.

**Devices they use:**
- **ALL-CAPS emphasis** on charged words mid-sentence: `BREATH`, `ALIVENESS`, `LIFE TRANSFORMATION`, `FREE GIFT`.
- **Inclusive pronouns** — "we", "us", "together" — to build community.
- **Direct invitations** — "Let's start together…", "Take a step towards…".
- **About-the-founder copy in third person**; brand philosophy in first-person plural ("we/our").

**Sample lines (verbatim):**
- "Tap into your most creative and alive self."
- "Let's start together this journey towards more inner freedom!"
- "Discover your inner power of transformation!"
- "Experience the power of the BREATH & learn how to guide others in reclaiming their ALIVENESS."
- "Take a step towards transforming your life and learning tools to support others' transformation."
- "Through mastering the mind and the relationship to the subconscious we can rise above false beliefs and limitations…"

**Do:** evocative, embodied, hopeful, second-person invitations.
**Avoid:** corporate/clinical phrasing, hard-sell hype, jargon without heart.
